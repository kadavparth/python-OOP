Title : Math Painting

Description:
Draw squares and rectangles on a blank canvas with given colors and coordinates from the user.
The program then makes a file to display the image with the given shapes

Objects:

Square:
    x
    y
    side
    color
    draw(canvas)
Rectangle:
    x
    y
    side
    color
    draw(canvas)
Canvas:
    width
    height
    color
Export:
    make(image)
